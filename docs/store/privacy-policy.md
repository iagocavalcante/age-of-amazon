# Age of Amazon — Privacy Policy

Age of Amazon does not collect personal data.

- No account, email, or sign-in exists.
- Online multiplayer uses a self-chosen public player name. It is stored
  with match results (wins, losses, rating) on our game server and shown
  on public leaderboards. It is not linked to your identity, device, or
  any other data.
- Single-player progress is stored only on your device.
- No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.
- Multiplayer network traffic goes to game.iagocavalcante.com solely to
  run matches.

To remove a player name from the leaderboards, open an issue at
https://github.com/iagocavalcante/age-of-amazon/issues.
